Preventive Measures

Here are essential steps to prevent cyberattacks:

  • Use Email Aliases – Protect your main email address by using aliases to avoid exposing your actual address to the world.
  • Create Strong Passwords – Ensure all accounts have unique, complex passwords to reduce vulnerabilities.
  • Enable Second-Factor Authentication – Add an extra layer of security to your accounts by requiring a second verification step.
