Email Aliases: Protect Your Privacy

Your email address is a gateway to your personal information. Exposing it publicly can lead to:

  • Increased spam and phishing attempts.
  • Unauthorized access attempts to your accounts.
  • Unwanted tracking and profiling by marketers.

To safeguard your privacy, consider using email aliases. An alias is an alternative email address that forwards messages to your primary inbox, allowing you to:

  • Keep your real email address hidden.
  • Manage and filter incoming emails more effectively.
  • Identify and control sources of unwanted emails.
