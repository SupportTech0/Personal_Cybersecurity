Online Tools for Detecting Suspicious Links and Scams

Utilize the following tools to analyze links and detect potential scams:

VirusTotal

VirusTotal allows you to check suspicious links and files against multiple antivirus engines to detect malware and phishing content.

Bitdefender Scamio

Bitdefender Scamio is an AI-powered scam detector where you can share texts and links to identify potential scams. It's free and accessible via web, WhatsApp, Messenger, and Discord.

EasyDMARC Phishing URL Checker

EasyDMARC's Phishing URL Checker helps you detect malicious links in real-time, ensuring you avoid phishing scams and malicious webpages.

F-Secure Link Checker

F-Secure Link Checker allows you to check if a link is safe to open, helping you avoid malicious websites and online scams.

NordVPN Link Checker

NordVPN's Link Checker is a free tool to verify if a URL is safe, helping you avoid phishing, malware, and other online threats.

CheckPhish

CheckPhish is a real-time URL scanner providing deep threat intelligence, including screenshots, certificates, and hosting details, to detect fraudulent sites.

IPQualityScore Malicious URL Scanner

IPQualityScore's Malicious URL Scanner checks URLs for phishing, malware, viruses, and reputation issues to prevent access to dangerous websites.
